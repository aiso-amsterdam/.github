# AISO Amsterdam

**The largest AI student organization in the Netherlands.**

We're the leading AI student society in the Netherlands. We host events, run workshops, ship projects, and
connect students with industry and academia across the Amsterdam AI ecosystem.

---

## What we do

- 🎤 **Speaker events** with researchers and operators from leading AI companies
- 🛠 **Workshops & hackathons** on applied ML, LLMs, and AI engineering
- 💻 **AI Projects** built by our student team 
- 🤝 **Industry connections** for internships, research, and full-time roles

## Some of our collabortators & partners:

NVIDIA · JetBrains · Miro · ING · Prosus · Microsoft · Microsoft · ABN Amro · UNICRI · ML6 · Perplexity
